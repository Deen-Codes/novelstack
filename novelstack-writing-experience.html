Design decisions baked into this

  • Immersive editor. The app nav and bottom tab bar collapse the moment the keyboard appears, and return on keyboard-down — focus on the page, no fake fullscreen.
  • Restrained, tasteful formatting. Italic, bold, chapter heading, a scene-break divider, and block quote — that's the whole set. No font pickers, no colour, no size sliders: the platform owns typography so every book reads consistently and looks premium.
  • Two toolbars. A persistent bar above the keyboard for insert-image / scene-break / bold / italic / heading / undo, plus a Medium-style selection bubble that appears only when text is selected.
  • Inline illustrations. Block-level images with a caption and alt text, drag-to-reorder — for illustrated chapters.
  • Reader-identical rendering. Chapters are stored as a structured block document (paragraph / heading / scene-break / image / quote with inline marks), not raw HTML — safe, and one shared renderer means a writer's formatting looks byte-identical to every reader, on mobile and web.
  • Draft → Scheduled → Live. Every chapter has a status; writers publish now or schedule ahead; autosave never loses a word; followers get a notification on publish.
